Essential Series

If you’re interested in a more in-depth culinary experience, our comprehensive Essential Series program is a great start. We offer two Certification courses—the Baking and Pastry Arts Series (EBPS), and the (ECS). Both use a techniques-based curriculum with a hands-on experience that are designed to equip you with the know-how to utilize your craft and talents professionally. Our acclaimed instructors will teach you the basic theoretical foundations so that by the end of this series, you will be able to develop your own recipes and build a strong practical skill set in the kitchen.

Our Essential Series is an intensive 22 or 23-day program. We offer two modules for the schedule of the Essential Series: the weekday module with classes held every Monday, Tuesday and Wednesday; and the weekend module with classes held every Saturday.

Every course ends with a culminating event or the graduation, where students will get to prepare all the meals for a celebratory lunch or dinner with their respective guests. The graduation is usually an emotional last day, as the students put their newly learned skills to the test, and say goodbye to the new friends that they have gained along the way. On this day, we give the Silver Spoon Award to deserving outstanding students who have shown an undying passion and impressive growth in their performance throughout the course.

Baking series

Baking, cake decorating or even confectionery making is a profession based on science and passion.

With our  Essentials for Baking and Pastry series, we make sure to teach you all the skills you need to know.  And at some point, we inspire you to make the science of baking into an art that is originally your own.

Cooking series

Aside from wet & dry cooking techniques, the certificate course delivers on necessary subjects like sanitation, mise en place, knife skills and basic baking and cake decorating.

An elaborate graduation is venue for skills application, menu planning. Certificates and recommendations for on the job training (OJT) are given to students who will fully complete the course.
