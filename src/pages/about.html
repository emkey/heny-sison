About Us

Driven by founder Heny Sison’s sheer love for the craft and sharing her passion with others, we formally established a school of Cake Decorating and Baking in 1985. We were born from humble beginnings, from a converted garage where friends and neighbors would drop by to take classes. Since then, the school has expanded its course offerings to a significantly wider range of culinary and lifestyle programs. In 2003, we were eventually renamed as the Heny Sison Culinary School, now a nationally recognized, full-fledged culinary institution.

Who We Are

We are a boutique school, a specialized culinary enterprise that is brimming with inspiration and integrity. Being so, we ensure that our teaching methods are highly personalized by maintaining an intimate, student-centered and a fun-loving learning environment.

We take pride in the fact that many of our graduates come back to us with stories of success, as they become business entrepreneurs and chefs on their own, making waves domestically and internationally.

What we believe in

Going beyond just refining the necessary skill sets required in the culinary field, we are committed to providing people with a lifetime objective—on setting visions to always looking forward, deepening understanding, and broadening horizons. These are the standards that we uphold. We ensure that all our courses are at par with the best, and that our curriculum is constantly updated.

What we offer

We want to teach you how to create environments that spark connections, by bringing people together through food. In the process of learning, you will appreciate what you can create in the kitchen, with a passion.

Whether you’re just a beginner, or are looking to hone an existing talent: we offer both short courses through our Lifestyle Classes, as well as intensive courses through our Essential Series, both of which cover the full range from recipe-based lessons to the more advanced techniques.
